Frame Drop Interactive is an independent game studio from Wayanad, Kerala — bringing a new voice to narrative gaming.

We believe in games that hit like cinema. Tight mechanics. Relentless atmosphere. Characters that breathe. Every pixel is a creative decision.

We're early. We're lean. And we're building something that will stand alongside the greats.

Our Code

Three Pillars

01
Narrative First
Every mechanic serves the story. We start with what we want players to feel — and engineer backwards from there. Systems exist to amplify emotion, not replace it.
02
No Compromise
We don't ship until it's right. Deadlines bend before vision does. We take the time to make something that earns a place in a player's memory — permanently.
03
Cinematic Craft
We study film. We study architecture. We study sound design. Our games are built with the obsession of a director — every frame composed, every beat timed.
